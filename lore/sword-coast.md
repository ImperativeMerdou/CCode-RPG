# THE SWORD COAST — the map beyond the walls

North to south along the Trackless Sea. Travel is caravan, ship or foot. Baldur's Gate to Waterdeep: ~40 days by caravan on the Coast Way / Trade Way, ~10 by ship with fair wind.

## North of the Gate
- **Waterdeep.** The City of Splendors. Biggest, richest city in the North. Masked Lords rule, Laeral Silverhand fronts. Under it: Undermountain and Skullport, a smugglers' port in the dark. Crime split between the Xanathar Guild and the Zhentarim, at war since 1491.
- **Neverwinter.** Rebuilt volcano-scarred city, Lord Protector Dagult Neverember, who was thrown out of Waterdeep and still acts like he owns both.
- **Luskan.** Pirate city of five ship-captains, quietly owned by Jarlaxle's Bregan D'aerthe. No law, only arrangements.
- **Icewind Dale.** Frozen frontier past the Spine of the World. Ten towns, cold gods, Drizzt country.
- **Candlekeep.** Fortress library-monastery. Entry price: a book they do not have. All knowledge, including dragon prophecy scholarship, sleeps there.

## South and east of the Gate
- **Elturgard.** Upriver theocracy whose capital Elturel went to the Hells and back in 1492. The Companion (a second sun) still hangs over it. Paladin patrols, refugee bleed.
- **Amn.** Merchant oligarchy south of the Cloud Peaks. Money worship with a government attached. Athkatla, its capital, makes the Gate look honest.
- **Calimshan.** Far south. Ancient genie-haunted empire, now trade princes. Origin of Little Calimshan's people.
- **Tethyr.** Kingdom between Amn and Calimshan, forever recovering from civil war.
- **The Western Heartlands.** The roads between everything: Elturel, Scornubel, Triel. Caravan country, bandit arithmetic.

## The far powers that reach the coast
- **Thay.** Necromancer magocracy across the continent, ruled by the lich Szass Tam. Red Wizard enclaves trade in everything banned.
- **The Underdark.** The world below every world: drow cities (Menzoberranzan), duergar, mind flayers, older things. Doors everywhere. None marked.
- **The Moonsea.** Zhentarim homeland, hard cities, black keeps.

## Why it matters to a climber
Fame travels the trade roads at caravan speed and the sea lanes at ship speed. A bounty printed in the Gate reaches Waterdeep in two tendays, Amn in three. Turf is city business; LEGEND is coast business. The Board (state/bounty.md) is coast-wide. To be worshipped, first be discussed in three cities.
