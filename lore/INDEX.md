# LORE LIBRARY

The Realms as published, not as half-remembered. Grep this before naming anything canon.

- `timeline.md` — what happened before now, and when now is
- `baldurs-gate.md` — the city: districts, law, crime, places, power
- `sword-coast.md` — the map beyond the walls
- `legends.md` — the named players alive in 1495 DR, with bounty-board context
- `gods-and-dragons.md` — the pantheon, the dragon gods, and the ascension precedents
- `factions.md` — who runs what, current stance, how they touch a Lower City climber

**Canon boundary:** published material runs through 1492 DR (the Absolute crisis).
Everything after is table canon: our extrapolation, marked in these files where it matters. Once written here, it is true.

**When the files come up empty:** fetch the Forgotten Realms Wiki (forgottenrealms.fandom.com) for the missing fact, then write what you learned into the right file. The library grows or the game repeats itself.
