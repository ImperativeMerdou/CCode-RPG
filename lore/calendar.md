# CALENDAR — the Realms keep time

The Calendar of Harptos: 12 months of 30 days, each month three tendays. Days of the tenday climax on **Godsday** (the 10th, 20th, 30th): markets peak, wages pay, the Guild collects, heat cools if the tenday stayed quiet.

## Months (with the street's read on each)
Hammer (deepwinter) | Alturiak (the claws of cold) | Ches (thaw mud) | Tarsakh (storms) | Mirtul (the melting) | Kythorn (green trade) | **Flamerule (NOW: heat, tempers, the bleeding month)** | Eleasis (highsun, drought) | Eleint (harvest, fat markets) | Marpenoth (rot and rain) | Uktar (the dying) | Nightal (the drawing down)

## Festival days (between months, no month owns them)
- **Midwinter** (after Hammer): nobles feast, the poor count firewood
- **Greengrass** (after Tarsakh): flowers, fertility, drunk druids
- **MIDSUMMER (after Flamerule, TEN DAYS FROM CAMPAIGN START):** the year's great feast. In the Gate: street tables, pit tournaments with fat purses, patriar masques, the Fist stretched thin, and every gang in the city working the crowds. Weddings by the hundred; Sune's and Tymora's clergy process. The best and worst night of the year to be ambitious.
- **Highharvestide** (after Eleint): harvest feast, roads fill with pilgrims
- **Feast of the Moon** (after Uktar): the dead are honored; Kelemvor's night; the Gate leaves candles at Candulhallow's
