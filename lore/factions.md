# FACTIONS — who runs what, 1495 DR

Each entry: what they are, what they want now, and the shape of first contact with a rising Lower City name.

## In the Gate
- **The Guild.** Nine-Fingers Keene's crime monopoly. Weakened 1492, meaner since. Kingpins under her hold territories; the Undercellar is her court. Wants: rebuild margins, crush independents early. First contact: a polite man with a ledger noting Merdou's earnings, once. There is no second note.
- **The Flaming Fist.** Mercenary police, Marshal Ravengard. Wants: order, contracts, no second Absolute. Bottom ranks bribable (2-5gp forgets a face), top ranks career-hungry. First contact: a shakedown or a recruitment offer, depending on his heat.
- **The Watch.** Upper City only. Wants: patriars sleeping well. Contact only if Merdou crosses the wall.
- **The patriars.** Forty families of old money. Wants: the Lower City profitable and invisible. Some fund Guild work through cutouts. First contact: an intermediary offering dirty work with clean gloves.
- **Elturel refugee rackets (table canon: "the Hellriders' Orphans").** Outer City veterans of the Descent running protection without Guild license. Wants: survival, spite, a wall of their own. Natural first allies or first meat for an ambitious independent.

## Coast-wide
- **The Zhentarim (the Black Network).** Mercenary-merchant syndicate. Sells swords, moves goods, buys men with debts. Wants: to be indispensable everywhere. First contact: an offer of work at fair pay, the hook hidden three favors deep.
- **The Harpers.** Spy network of meddlers for freedom's sake. Wants: no tyrants, which one day means Merdou. First contact: a bard who asks one question too many.
- **The Lords' Alliance.** The cities' mutual-defense pact (Waterdeep, the Gate, Neverwinter...). Prints the coast-wide bounty notices. Wants: stability. To them Merdou is paperwork that bleeds.
- **The Order of the Gauntlet.** Militant faith (Tyr, Torm, Helm). Wants: evil dead on schedule. They keep files on rising names with dark marks.
- **The Emerald Enclave.** Druids and rangers holding civilization and wild in balance. Mostly indifferent to city crime. A dragon being born in a city is not city crime.
- **The Cult of the Dragon.** Splintered fanatics who serve dragon supremacy: one wing raises dead dragons, one serves Tiamat, and (table canon) a third heresy hunts living prophecy-bearers, reading skin for Io's will. Wants: the Prophecy fulfilled through THEIR reading. The faction most dangerous to Merdou and most useful, in either order.
- **Bregan D'aerthe.** Jarlaxle's drow company. Wants: profit and novelty. They notice everything interesting on the coast eventually. Being noticed is the job interview.
- **The Xanathar Guild.** The beholder's reach into the Gate: fences, slavers, spice. At war with the Zhentarim in Waterdeep. Wants: everything, catalogued.
- **Red Wizards of Thay.** Enclave merchants and worse. Wants: relics, bodies, arcane leverage. They pay best and cost most. A prophecy mark would interest them clinically.
