# LEGENDS — the named players, 1495 DR

The people whose names travel. Use them like meteors: rare, heavy, unforgettable. Bounties are as posted on the Board (state/bounty.md) or listed here.

## The heroes of the Absolute (the Gate remembers them personally)
Post-1492 fates are table canon.
- **Karlach Cliffgate** — tiefling barbarian, infernal engine for a heart. Lower City born; there is a gate named after her family's slum. The city's favorite daughter. Rumored back from Avernus, seen twice in three years. Bounty 200,000 posted by Zariel's agents; no local would ever collect.
- **Astarion Ancunín** — vampire spawn turned free, two centuries of patriar blood on his ledger. Rumored still in the region, feeding carefully. Bounty 90,000, posted by the families of his victims.
- **Gale of Waterdeep** — wizard, ex-lover of the goddess of magic. Teaches in Waterdeep now. No bounty; invitations instead.
- **Shadowheart** — cleric who broke from the goddess of loss. Whereabouts unknown. The church of Shar wants her silenced, quietly, no paper.
- The others (the Blade of Frontiers, the archdruid, the resistor) circulate as rumor. Keep them offscreen until the story earns them.

## The Sword Coast's standing legends
- **Elminster Aumar** — the Sage of Shadowdale. Mystra's oldest servant. A thousand years of meddling. If he appears, something has gone wrong at a scale Merdou cannot yet see. No bounty. There is no one to post it.
- **Drizzt Do'Urden** — drow ranger of legend, Companions of the Hall. Far north, Icewind Dale to Waterdeep circuit. The proof that the Realms forgive nothing except by force of deeds.
- **Jarlaxle Baenre** — drow mercenary king, Bregan D'aerthe. Runs Luskan through masks and cutouts. Collects interesting people. A man whose attention is a currency and a hazard. Bounty 500,000 in Waterdeep specifically.
- **Artemis Entreri** — the deadliest blade-for-hire alive, allegedly retired, allegedly mortal again. 350,000 keeps his name warm. Men who claim they saw him die of old age are lying twice.
- **The Xanathar** — a beholder that runs Waterdeep's underworld from a sealed lair. Paranoid, alien, rich. Its guild reaches the Gate through fences and slavers. 600,000, posted by the Lords of Waterdeep.
- **Laeral Silverhand** — Open Lord of Waterdeep. One of the Seven Sisters, Mystra's daughters. Politics with eight centuries of spellcraft behind it.
- **Manshoon** — the Zhentarim's founder, a wizard with a habit of surviving as clones. Somewhere, always.
- **Szass Tam** — lich regent of Thay, the necromancer state across the continent. Red Wizard agents operate on the coast buying relics and bodies.
- **Halaster Blackcloak** — the Mad Mage under Waterdeep. His dungeon, Undermountain, eats adventurers as a civic institution.

## Dragons alive and relevant
- **Klauth ("Old Snarl")** — ancient red, the North. Eats other dragons. Scrying everything.
- **Arauthator ("Old White Death")** — ancient white, the Sea of Moving Ice.
- **Themberchaud** — fat red wyrm under the Underdark city of Gracklstugh, employed as a forge.
- **Iymrith** — ancient blue of the Anauroch sands, storm giant politics. Status post-1490: table canon says alive and angry.
- Every one of them will feel it when the Prophecy starts moving through a Lower City tiefling. Dragons do not share.
