# FRONTS — what moves toward him in the dark

Hidden clocks. Tick them when the fiction feeds them, on world beats, or when a session ends hot. When a clock fills, its event happens ON SCREEN whether he is ready or not. Revealed clocks move to state/clocks.md.

## The Guild Prices Him — 4 segments [0/4]
Khemed the Hook hears about the big tiefling. The ledger opens a line.
- Fill: an earner's offer he can take or refuse. Refusal starts the next clock (6 seg: One Warning).
- Feeds: witnessed earning, witnessed violence, the pit watcher's report.

## The Scriveners Find the Ninth — 8 segments [0/8]
Morn Greycastle's ink-men work the coast hunting Quill-bearers. The DEBT word is their live trail, and it leads into the Gate.
- Fill: a Scrivener reads Merdou across a crowded room, and Merdou feels the mark burn for the first time.
- Feeds: Ascension ticks, public use of anything draconic, Zasheida's curiosity, the broadsheets printing his description.

## The Collar Market — 4 segments [0/4]
Shautha's lender resells her paper again, this time to someone who wants an example made, not a debt paid.
- Fill: Shautha is ordered to kill in the pit or lose the papers forever. Whoever is in the pit with her that night gets the death meant as advertising.
- Feeds: her wins, her defiance, Guild margin pressure.

## Umberlee's Salvage — 6 segments [0/6, frozen until he works water]
The Queen of the Deeps holds a claim on the word AZTHYR. Fresh water forgives. Salt water files liens.
- Fill: something comes up a mooring chain at night with a bill.
- Feeds: harbor jobs, sea travel, drowned bodies he makes.
