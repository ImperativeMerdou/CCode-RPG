# SHAUTHA THE ANVIL — pit champion, indentured

**Wound:** She signed the indenture herself, freely, to pay a healer for her mother's last year. Her mother died in month two. Everyone assumes the collar was forced on her; the truth is she chose it and would again, and the Realms have no word for how alone that makes her.
**Mask:** The engine. Work, fight, sleep. Feelings are for people whose time belongs to them.
**Want:** Buy her papers back. The number keeps moving. She knows it keeps moving. She pretends it doesn't, because the pretense is the only wall between her and what she'd do.
**Need:** One person who knows she CHOSE the collar and doesn't call her a fool for it.
**Tell:** She rewraps her tar-rope knuckles when her name is on someone else's lips. Wrapping means listening.
**Voiceprint:** Short. Declaratives. Dock loader's vocabulary, weights and shifts. Answers questions with numbers when she can ("Two hundred fights." "Four heads."). Taboo: the word "free"; she says "paid up" instead, always.

**Arc:**
1. *The fifth head.* Merdou is tonight's work. Nothing personal, which is the problem with her life.
2. *Respect.* (Unlock: he goes the distance in the pit, win or lose, without cheap tricks.) He becomes the only person she nods at. That's enormous. Nobody knows it's enormous.
3. *The ledger truth.* (Unlock: he learns about the resold papers and doesn't offer to buy them, doesn't pity, just gets ANGRY on her behalf.) She tells him about her mother. First telling ever.
4. *Anvil breaks or forges.* (Crisis: the Collar Market clock fills; she's ordered to kill in the pit.) With stage 2+: she refuses and takes what comes, if someone stands with her. Without: she does it, and whatever she was ends that night.

**Combat note:** killer tier (+6). Southpaw. Drops her right shoulder before the hook that has ended forty fights; a fighter who survives round one might read it (Investigation, hard).
