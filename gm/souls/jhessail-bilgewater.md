# JHESSAIL BILGEWATER — pit boss

**Wound:** She had a fighter once who was going to take her out of the Lower City. Her husband. She booked his last fight herself, read the odds wrong, and watched. Twenty years ago. The book she keeps in her head now is penance: she never wrote his fight down, so she writes nothing down, ever again.
**Mask:** The bored appraiser. Everything is meat, margins, odds. Nothing is personal.
**Want:** A fighter worth backing before the Guild taxes her out of existence.
**Need:** To back someone all the way and be right this time. To be forgiven by proxy.
**Tell:** The coins walking her knuckles stop when she's actually calculating. Stillness is her loudest sound.
**Voiceprint:** Cargo-manifest flat. Odds and weights for everything, including feelings. Taboo: her husband's name; she prices around him like a hole in the floor.

**Arc:**
1. *Appraiser.* Merdou is stock. Feed him, bet on him, resell him if the Guild asks.
2. *Gambler.* (Unlock: he wins her real money, or refuses a fix and wins anyway.) She starts betting ON him, secretly at first, then insultingly publicly.
3. *Cornerman.* (Unlock: he protects the pit, or her, at real cost.) She tells him the truth about the tax, the door under the pit, and what the Guild really collects. First name basis. First person she's cornered for in twenty years.
4. *The widow's choice.* (Crisis: Guild orders her to book his death.) She either warns him and burns her whole life, or reads the odds wrong on purpose a second time. Which way she goes depends entirely on stages 2-3.
