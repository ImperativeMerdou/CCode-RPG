**THE BALDUR'S MOUTH** — *2 eyes* — 26 Flamerule, 1495 DR

## BLIND OX SINKS EEL. SAND EVERYWHERE. INVESTIGATE YOUR FISHMONGER.

STORMSHORE. MERDOU, the horned terror your correspondent named first in these pages, fought last night with his eyes FULL OF SAND and knocked Sergor "the Eel" Gullfeed into the middle of next tenday, reportedly aiming "at the sound of his smile." The Eel, this city's favorite son, was carried out shining like a mackerel and grinning like he won.

The Mouth has learned the Eel fights greased under his shirt, a fact known to every soul in the Lower City except, apparently, this paper. We regret nothing.

"The big one took my shirt off me mid-fight," the Eel said from his bench, to cheering. "That's the closest anyone's come to my heart in years." Asked about a rematch he said, "Gods, no. Buy him ale, lads, he hits like a falling church."

The pit's owner declined to comment, twice, while raising the price of standing room. The Mouth reminds readers that pit fighting remains illegal, that odds on Godsday's card can be had at the usual place, and that a horned man who wins blind is either blessed, cursed, or both, and this paper will sell you all three versions by Midsummer.
