**THE BALDUR'S MOUTH** — *2 eyes* — 21 Flamerule, 1495 DR

## MAD OX WRECKS BILGEWATER PIT, EATS FAMOUS LEFT, SLEEPS LIKE BABY

STORMSHORE. Under a certain fish-house last night, a half-orc dockhand of unusual size, named by patrons as one MALARK EVENWOOD, lately off a grain coaster, threw the pit favorite Keth "the Brick" through three tables and one bookmaker. This paper counted the tables itself this morning. There was one table. Patrons insist on three.

"He roared so loud my ale went flat," swears a witness who gave his name as Nobody. "There were two of him," swears another, who had enjoyed the storm indoors since noon.

The giant then addressed SHAUTHA THE ANVIL, unbeaten queen of the low pits, in language this paper will repeat at the counter for one copper extra. The Anvil answered with the left hand known to every surgeon on the docks. Asked afterward for comment on her fifth win of the night, she said, in full, "Five."

The Mouth reminds its readers that pit fighting remains illegal in Baldur's Gate, and that odds on the next card can be had at the usual place by asking for nobody in particular. SPEAK WITH DEAD, discreet, fair rates: ask for the blue door at the Elfsong.
