# SEALED PROPHECIES — the GM swears the future in advance

Each prophecy is written, sealed in the vault, and its fingerprint (SHA-256) is published here on the day it is sworn. When one comes true on screen, the sealed text is revealed and anyone can hash the file to prove it was written before the events, not after. No retcons. No "I planned that all along." The math is the witness.

Verify anytime: `sha256sum gm/sealed/prophecy-NN.md`
