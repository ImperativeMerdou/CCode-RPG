# THE MARK — between his shoulder blades, where he cannot see

One word in Draconic. It reads only mirrored, by firelight. He has never read it.
At each Ascension tick, another stroke surfaces. This file is the skin.

Faint raised lines, like an old burn seen by feel. A dock tattooist once
offered to ink over it and put the needle down without explaining himself.
It itches in three circumstances he has not connected: open flame near his
back, the harbor at low tide, and once, four years ago, for a whole night,
when something enormous flew over the city too high to see.
